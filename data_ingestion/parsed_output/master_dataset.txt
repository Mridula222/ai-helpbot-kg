
=== mosdac_faqs.txt ===
Q1: What is MOSDAC?
A1: MOSDAC stands for Meteorological and Oceanographic Satellite Data Archival Centre by ISRO.

Q2: What satellites are supported?
A2: INSAT-3D, SCATSAT-1, Oceansat-2, Oceansat-3, etc.

Q3: Is MOSDAC data free?
A3: Yes, most datasets are free to access after registration.

Q4: What formats are used?
A4: NetCDF, HDF, GeoTIFF, and standard image formats.

Q5: How can I register?
A5: Visit the registration section on MOSDAC and provide your credentials.
